### Q: What if I transfer to the wrong account?
**A:** Contact support immediately with:
- Your booking reference
- Transaction receipt/reference
- Amount transferred
- Account used

### Q: How long does the transfer take?
**A:** 
- Same bank transfers: 5-30 minutes
- Inter-bank transfers: 1-2 hours
- International transfers: 1-3 business days

### Q: What's the booking reference for?
**A:** The booking reference:
- Identifies your booking in our system
- Should be included in transfer memo
- Helps us match payments to bookings quickly

### Q: Can I transfer from abroad?
**A:** Yes, but:
- Use the SWIFT code (e.g., ABNGNGLA)
- International transfers may take 1-3 days
- Check with your bank for international transfer fees

### Q: What if the amount doesn't match?
**A:** The system will reject mismatched payments. Ensure:
- Amount matches exactly (including decimals)
- Currency matches the selected account
- No extra fees added (transfer should be net)

### Q: Is my booking confirmed immediately?
**A:** No, confirmation happens when:
1. Payment received in our account
2. Booking verified in our system
3. Confirmation email sent (usually within 2 hours)
